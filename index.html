Youth Divergent
& AdvoKada



Bringing youth to God's vision

Youth Divergent is a youth organization where we help guide young people towards God's vision from various school campuses, empowering them to build better relationships with God and others.


Youth Divergent is a service in TGBTG Church Mabuhay Outreach where students from any school campuses gather to learn the word of God.

AdvoKada is a youth organization which support DepEd's core values. It helps students learn the word of God in an educational manner.
